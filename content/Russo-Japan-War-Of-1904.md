The war began due to a fight for control of Manchuria. Russia wanted an evergreen port on their east cost.

[Tsar](Tsar-Nicholas-II) thought that the war was a good idea. Victory would gain support of the people.

However, it caused inflation, shortages of food and goods, and lack of industrial materials.

The Russians were also heavily defeated by the Japanese.

Port Author fell to the Japanese in 1905.
